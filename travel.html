Travel

We recommend staying in downtown Portland. The dinner cruise will embark from the downtown waterfront. OMSI is located on the east side of the river, about a 5-10 minute drive from downtown Portland. Here are a few of the hotels we would recommend:

  • Marriott Portland Waterfront
  • Embassy Suites Portland Downtown
  • Courtyard by Marriott Portland City Center
  • Hotel Modera use the code “PRO15” in order to get the 15% off discount.
  • Hotel deLuxe 15% discount if you call to make reservation. 866-986-8085
  • Hotel Lucia 15% discount if you call to make a reservation at 866-986-8086
